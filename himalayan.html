About Himalaya Cloth Brand Shewa Adda

Established in 2005, Himalaya Cloth Brand has been serving the community of Swabi with premium quality traditional clothing. Our store in Shewa Adda has become a trusted destination for those who appreciate fine craftsmanship and authentic Pakistani designs.

We take pride in offering garments that combine traditional aesthetics with modern comfort, using only the finest fabrics sourced from across Pakistan.
